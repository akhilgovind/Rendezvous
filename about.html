about us

EXPLORE THE BEAUTY OF
WAYANAD

Isle Caddy invites you to make your days more beautiful & closer to Nature This beautiful resort is situated on a peninsula surrounded by the water of famed dam of Karapuzha. The ambience in this very water-front property is so splendid with the thick Palms, dense garden of Fruits and Medicinal Plants. The tent accommodation offered at the banks of the dam inside the property is incredibly awesome. The resort stands facing the Karapuzha Lake far away from the city life vices in a tranquil setting where you enjoy a perfect retreat to plunge into the profundity of natural ecstasy. Properly trained professional staffs maintain a pleasant attitude towards the guests here and all your necessities are catered in the best possible way. Entire room design is tweaked for maximum luxury, comfort and privacy for our guests. There is a 24 hour power backup and all modern amenities are availed.To make your stay more memorable, lip smacking cuisines of different types like South Indian, Traditional Malabar, North Indian, continental and Chinese cuisines areserved. We use only organically cultivated vegetables and whole cooking process is carried out perfectly in harmless way.
